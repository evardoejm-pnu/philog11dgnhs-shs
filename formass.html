Final Review

You have completed the module. Remember these key distinctions:

  • Correspondence: Truth matches physical facts.
  • Coherence: Truth matches an internal logical system.
  • Pragmatic: Truth is found in what works or is useful.
  • Deontology: Justification based on duty/rules.
  • Consequentialism: Justification based on results/outcomes.
